The javelin throw requires a combination of several skill and health-related components of fitness.

    Skill-related components:

    • Coordination:

      Javelin throwers need to coordinate their movements to generate maximum power and precision during the throw.

    • Speed:

      The ability to generate speed during the run-up and transfer it effectively to the javelin is crucial.

    • Accuracy:

      A high level of accuracy is essential to ensure the javelin lands at the desired distance and angle.>

  1. Health-related components:

    • Muscular strength and power:

      Strong and powerful muscles, especially in the upper body and core, are important for generating the force required to propel the javelin

    • Flexibility:

      Good flexibility in the shoulders, arms, and trunk is necessary to achieve the optimal throwing position and to prevent injury.

    • Cardiovascular endurance:

      A good level of cardiovascular fitness is important for the run-up and the explosive effort of the throw.
